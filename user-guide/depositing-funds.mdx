Depositing funds into MetaVault AI allows you to participate in AI-powered yield generation strategies. Your LINK tokens are managed by smart contracts and allocated across multiple DeFi protocols to maximize returns.

## Prerequisites

<Check>
  Your wallet must be connected. See [Connecting Your Wallet](/user-guide/connecting-wallet) first.
</Check>

<Check>
  You need LINK tokens in your wallet. You can mint test LINK tokens from the dashboard.
</Check>

<Check>
  You need Sepolia ETH for gas fees.
</Check>

## Getting Test LINK Tokens

Before depositing, you'll need LINK tokens. MetaVault AI provides a convenient way to mint test LINK tokens:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Quick Actions">
    In the dashboard, locate the **Quick Actions** card below the stats grid.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 'Mint Mock LINK'">
    Click the **"Mint Mock LINK"** button with the yellow background. This will mint 100 LINK tokens to your wallet.
    
    ![Mint Mock LINK button appears with dollar sign icon and yellow background](/images/user-guide/mint-link-button.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve in Wallet">
    Your wallet will prompt you to approve the mint transaction. Review and confirm the transaction.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    The button will show "Minting..." while the transaction processes. Once complete, you'll see a green success message.
    
    Your LINK balance will update automatically in the top navigation bar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can also copy the LINK token address using the **"Copy LINK Address"** button to add the token to your wallet or get LINK from other sources.
</Tip>

## Depositing LINK Tokens

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Deposit Modal">
    In the dashboard, click the blue **"Deposit"** button in the Quick Actions section.
    
    ![Deposit button with blue gradient background in Quick Actions card](/images/user-guide/deposit-button.png)
    
    The Deposit modal will open, displaying:
    - Amount input field
    - Your current LINK balance
    - Approve & Deposit / Deposit button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Deposit Amount">
    Type the amount of LINK tokens you want to deposit in the input field.
    
    ![Deposit modal shows amount input field with your LINK balance displayed below](/images/user-guide/deposit-modal-input.png)
    
    Your available balance is displayed below the input field for reference.
    
    <Warning>
      Make sure you don't exceed your available LINK balance. The interface will show an "Insufficient balance" error if you do.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Approve Token Spending (First Time Only)">
    For your first deposit, the vault contract needs permission to spend your LINK tokens. Click **"Approve & Deposit"**.
    
    This triggers two transactions:
    1. **Approval transaction**: Grants the vault permission to spend LINK
    2. **Deposit transaction**: Automatically executes after approval
    
    ![Button shows 'Approve & Deposit' for first-time deposits](/images/user-guide/approve-deposit-button.png)
    
    <Note>
      After the first approval, future deposits will only require one transaction and the button will show **"Deposit"** instead.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm in Wallet">
    Your wallet will prompt you to:
    1. First, approve the token spending limit
    2. Then, confirm the deposit transaction
    
    Review each transaction carefully and approve both.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for Confirmation">
    The button will update to show:
    - **"Approving..."** during token approval
    - **"Depositing..."** during the deposit transaction
    
    ![Loading state shows 'Depositing...' on the button](/images/user-guide/deposit-loading.png)
    
    Wait for both transactions to complete. This typically takes 10-30 seconds on Sepolia.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Your Deposit">
    Once successful:
    - The modal closes automatically
    - Your dashboard stats update in real-time:
      - **Your Shares** increases by your deposited amount
      - **Your Assets** reflects your vault balance
      - **Total Managed** increases for the entire vault
    
    ![Updated dashboard stats show increased shares and assets](/images/user-guide/deposit-success-stats.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Your Shares

When you deposit LINK tokens:

- You receive **vault shares** representing your ownership percentage
- Shares are ERC-20 tokens that track your proportional claim to vault assets
- As the vault generates yield, your shares become worth more LINK
- The share-to-asset ratio changes as the vault earns returns

**Example:**
- You deposit 100 LINK
- You receive 100 shares
- Vault earns 10% return
- Your 100 shares are now worth 110 LINK

## Real-Time Updates

MetaVault AI uses real-time event listeners to update your dashboard instantly when:

- Your deposit is confirmed
- Other users deposit or withdraw
- The AI rebalances strategies
- Yield is harvested from protocols

You don't need to refresh the page - all stats update automatically.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Transaction failed - Insufficient balance">
    This means you're trying to deposit more LINK than you have. Check your LINK balance in:
    - The deposit modal (shows current balance)
    - The top navigation bar
    - Your wallet
    
    Reduce your deposit amount or mint more test LINK.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Approval transaction succeeded but deposit didn't start">
    The deposit should trigger automatically after approval. If it doesn't:
    1. Close and reopen the deposit modal
    2. Try depositing again (you won't need to approve again)
    3. Check your wallet for any pending transaction prompts
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Transaction is taking a long time">
    Sepolia testnet can have variable block times:
    - Wait up to 2-3 minutes for confirmation
    - Check the transaction status in your wallet
    - Look up the transaction hash on [Sepolia Etherscan](https://sepolia.etherscan.io/)
    
    Don't submit the transaction again or you may deposit twice.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Stats aren't updating after deposit">
    The dashboard uses real-time event listeners, but if stats don't update:
    1. Wait 10-15 seconds for block confirmation
    2. Check that the transaction completed successfully
    3. Refresh the page if needed
    
    The automatic refresh interval is 3 seconds for most stats.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I rejected the transaction by mistake">
    No problem! Simply:
    1. Click the Deposit button again
    2. Enter the same or different amount
    3. Approve the transaction this time
    
    No funds were transferred if you rejected the transaction.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
